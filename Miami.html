Miami, Florida

I want to visit Miami for its vibrant beaches, Art Deco architecture, and Latin-American cultural fusion. From South Beach to Little Havana, Miami offers a unique blend of sun, sea, and city life.

How to Travel to Miami, Florida

  • Flight: Book a flight from Manila (MNL) to Miami International Airport (MIA) with layovers in Los Angeles, San Francisco, or New York (approx. 18-22 hours).
  • Best Time to Visit: November to April for warm weather and minimal rainfall.
  • Must-Visit: South Beach, Wynwood Walls, Vizcaya Museum, and Everglades National Park.
