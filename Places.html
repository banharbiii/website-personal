Where i wanna be in the future

Utah, USA

Utah is a southwestern U.S. state known for its stunning red rock landscapes, the Great Salt Lake, and being the headquarters of the Mormon Church (The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints).

Paris, France

Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its iconic Eiffel Tower, world-class art museums like the Louvre, rich cultural heritage, elegant architecture, and status as a global center of fashion, cuisine, and romantic ambiance.

New York, USA

New York City, famously known as the "Big Apple," is a vibrant hub of culture, finance, and entertainment. Renowned for landmarks like the Statue of Liberty, Times Square, and Central Park, its a city of endless energy, diversity, and innovation that draws millions of visitors and dreamers from around the globe.

Maldives

The Maldives, a breathtaking archipelago in the Indian Ocean, boasts turquoise lagoons, world-class overwater villas, vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life, and offers an unparalleled escape into serenity and luxury.

Miami, USA

Miami, USA, is a dynamic coastal gem celebrated for its sun-soaked beaches, buzzing nightlife, vibrant cultural diversity, iconic Art Deco architecture, thriving arts scene, and a unique fusion of Latin and American influences that create an electric, colorful atmosphere.
