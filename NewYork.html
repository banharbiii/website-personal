How to go to New York, USA

To travel from the Philippines to New York, USA, book a flight from Manila (MNL) to John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK) or Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR). The journey typically involves a layover in major hubs like Hong Kong or Tokyo (18-20 hours total). Ensure you have a valid US visa, passport, and return ticket. From the airport, use the AirTrain, subway, or a taxi to reach the heart of Manhattan.
