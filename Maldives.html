Maldives

I dream of visiting the Maldives for its pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and luxurious overwater bungalows. It's the ultimate tropical paradise for relaxation and underwater adventures.

How to Travel to Maldives

  • Flight: Book a flight from Manila (MNL) to Malé (MLE) with layovers in Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, or Doha (approx. 12-16 hours total travel time).
  • Seaplane/Transfer: From Malé International Airport, take a speedboat or seaplane transfer to your resort island (30 minutes to 1 hour).
  • Best Time: Visit between November and April for dry season with calm seas and best visibility for snorkeling/diving.
