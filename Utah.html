How to go to Utah, USA

To go from the Philippines to Utah, USA, take a flight from Manila (MNL) to Salt Lake City (SLC) with a layover in Los Angeles (LAX) or San Francisco (SFO) (18-24 hours total), make sure you have a valid US visa, passport, and return ticket, then ride the TRAX light rail or rent a car from SLC airport to your destination.
